3D_Printer

Underverk's 3D printer is a fork of a wooden Prusa i3 made by Arduino LLC for the 3D printing workshops to be held at FBRKN, STPLN, Malmo, Sweden. It's playfully called the "i3 AV Remix"

It's a mashup of parts from:

  • Modified Parts from the EiNSTeiN branch
  • The Prusa vanilla repo
  • The extruder is based on Misans version of the compact extruder
  • The frame is based on Shane grabers Laser Cut 6mm Wood frame
  • And x-carriage was inspired by Shane grabers Graber printer
  • A modified version of this y-motor-mount
